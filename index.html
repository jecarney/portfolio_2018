ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Jules, a Toronto-based full-stack developer. I got into coding because I have an endless fascination with the art and science of writing elegant logic, and because I like building interactive projects that people really use.

For the past several years, I've been working as a developer in the telecommunications industry, but am currently transitioning into web development. My strong suits:

  • I have four years of experience with JavaScript and related libraries and frameworks, such as React and jQuery.
  • I'm a versatile coder with professional experience building software from database, to server-side, to front end.
  • I have a diverse skill base that includes Object-oriented Programming, HTML and CSS, and Geographic Information Systems.

In my life outside of code, I like to play and listen to music, read up on the latest in science, and play games.
